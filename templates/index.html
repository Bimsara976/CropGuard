Detectable Conditions

CropGuard currently detects four conditions in pumpkin/cucurbit leaves:

Downy Mildew

Caused by Pseudoperonospora cubensis. High-severity fungal-like disease.

Leaf Curl Disease

Viral disease transmitted by whiteflies. Causes severe yield loss.

Mosaic Virus

CMV or WMV spread by aphids. Reduces fruit quality and marketability.

Healthy Leaf

Confirms no disease is detected and plant appears healthy.
